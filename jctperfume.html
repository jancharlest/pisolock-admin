Why Oil

Pure fragrance,
nothing wasted.

Alcohol-free formula means every drop goes directly into your skin — not into the air.

All-Day Longevity

Oil-based fragrances bond directly to skin and last 8–16 hours — significantly longer than alcohol-based sprays that evaporate within hours.

Skin-Deep Scent

The oil base absorbs slowly into skin, releasing scent throughout the day in a natural, evolving way — never harsh, never overwhelming.

Gentle Formula

No alcohol means no drying effect on skin. Perfect for sensitive skin types, and safe to apply directly to pulse points for maximum effect.

Affordable Luxury

Premium fragrance quality without the luxury price tag. A small amount goes a long way — making it one of the most cost-effective scent choices.

Travel-Friendly

Compact, non-pressurized, and spill-safe. Bring it anywhere without worrying about airport restrictions or accidental spray in your bag.

Pure Concentration

Higher fragrance oil concentration than EDT or EDC sprays. More scent per drop, better projection, and a more complex, authentic aroma.
